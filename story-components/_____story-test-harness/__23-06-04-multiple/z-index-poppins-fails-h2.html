Like all food commodities, wheat has its issues

Growers

It can be the victim of different diseases and bugs

Bakers

Very particular about about varietis of wheat and properties of flour

Supply chain

Must protect wheat from decay and predators

Consumers

It has a lot of nutritious value, but it is also known as the source of gluten, which can cause serious health issues to celiacs and other groups
